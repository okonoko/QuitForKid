Checklist

Useful things to do

Write down why you want to quit and put it somewhere visible, the more honest and important for you answer will have a better effect

Set a date. Hard date, not one that you say you will try. Set a date and tell your friends/family that will take you accountable

Surround yourself with people that will support you. People that won’t offer you cigarette, or will remind you that you want to quit

If your partner is a smoker, convincing him to stop with you will highly increase your chances. If he decides not to quit, don’t worry You can totally do it on your own.

Find someone not jugmental who you can talk to about your struggles. Try to find this person non smoker

Figure out your triggers, and find new habbit to replace your curving at those times. Break the pattern.

Don’t give up. Even when you fail. Many people relaps and afterwards they quit forever. You probably relapsed quite a few time. So hang it there and remember why you do it
